Soil Testing Guide

When to Test

  • Before planting new gardens
  • Every 2-3 years for established areas
  • When plants show nutrient deficiency
  • After major soil amendments

What to Test

  • pH level
  • Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium
  • Organic matter content
  • Micronutrients (if needed)

How to Sample

  • Take samples from multiple locations
  • Sample at 6-8 inches depth
  • Mix samples together
  • Send to certified lab
