üß™ pH Adjustment Guide

To Raise pH (Make Less Acidic):

  • Add lime (calcium carbonate)
  • Use wood ash (small amounts)
  • Apply dolomitic limestone

To Lower pH (Make More Acidic):

  • Add sulfur
  • Use aluminum sulfate
  • Apply organic mulches

Key Terms:

  • Aggregation: Soil particles bound together

  • Humus: Stable organic matter
  • Leaching: Nutrient loss through water
  • Tilth: Physical condition of soil

üî¨ DIY Soil Tests

Jar Test (Texture):

  • Fill jar 1/3 with soil
  • Add water and shake
  • Let settle 24 hours
  • Observe layers: sand, silt, clay

Drainage Test:

  • Dig 12-inch hole
  • Fill with water
  • Time how long to drain
  • Good: 1-3 hours
