Soil Type Quick Reference

Sandy Soil

Best for: Root vegetables (carrots, radish), peanuts, melons, herbs

Clay Soil

Best for: Rice, broccoli, cabbage, fruit trees, ornamentals

Loam Soil

Best for: Most crops - wheat, corn, tomatoes, beans, vegetables

Silt Soil

Best for: Grasses, bamboo, wetland crops, moisture-loving plants
